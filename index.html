About Me

I’m Mustahid Hasan, a dedicated Software Engineer with a strong foundation in Python, Selenium, and Django. Currently, I work remotely at Brandcloud, Inc., where I focus on automating software processes, developing RESTful APIs, and managing projects using Jira. My expertise also includes working with Docker and AWS, building efficient and scalable systems for various automation tasks. I’m passionate about solving complex problems and continuously learning new technologies. With a Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Daffodil International University, my academic interests include AI, NLP, and machine learning. I enjoy applying these skills in real-world applications to drive innovation and efficiency.
